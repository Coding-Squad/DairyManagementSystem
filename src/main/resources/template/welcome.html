Calcium is also important in the prevention of bone loss in women and bone mass development in all ages.

Cheese

Cheese is a dairy product derived from milk that is produced in a wide range of flavors, textures, and forms by coagulation of the milk protein casein. It comprises proteins and fat from milk, usually cows' milk. Cheese is valued for its portability, long life, and high content of fat, protein, calcium, and phosphorus. Cheese is more compact and has a longer shelf life than milk, although how long a cheese will keep depends on the type of cheese; labels on packets of cheese often claim that a cheese should be consumed within three to five days of opening.

Butter

Butter is a dairy product containing up to 80% butterfat which is solid when chilled and at room temperature in some regions and liquid when warmed. It is made by churning fresh or fermented cream or milk to separate the butterfat from the buttermilk. It is generally used as a spread on plain or toasted bread products and a condiment on cooked vegetables, as well as in cooking, such as baking, sauce making, and pan frying. Butter consists of butterfat, milk proteins and water, and in some types, added salt. Butter may also be sold with added flavourings, such as garlic butter.

Factory Background

Egerton Dairy factory is located in Njoro Nakuru county, kenya.It is inside Egerton university. The factory was started in the year 2001 as a laboratory where Egerton students taking bachelor in Dairy and Food sciences would take their practical work and do their research work. This was primary reason why it was made.



The Management

As the years went by,it grew into a bigger factory, as the number of students,staff and employees increased yearly.It was later made a department of its own within the university.Though its activity has increased over the the years, it primary goal is still highly regaded ,to serve students.

The factory is being managed by the General manager who is under the Dean of students in the faculty of Dairy Technology.The general manager is responsible for the management of the factory, he oversees the day to day activity of the the factory, then gives the report to the Dean. The Dean on the other hand checks the afairs of the students in relation to the factory and the general welfare of students taking the course.
